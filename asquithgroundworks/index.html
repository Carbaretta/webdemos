Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about access, materials, or timelines? We lay out clear answers below.

We are based in Liversedge, West Yorkshire (WF15 7BD). We primarily serve Cleckheaton, Bradford, Leeds, Heckmondwike, Batley, Huddersfield, Halifax, and surrounding districts. For large civil works or commercial groundworks, we can travel further across West Yorkshire.

Groundwork and drainage require heavy plant machinery (excavators, skip loaders, compactors). Easy access means we can roll in 3-tonne or 5-tonne machines directly, speeding up excavation. Restricted access (like a standard garden gate or steps) forces us to use micro-diggers or perform manual digging, which increases the labor timeline and project cost.

We never cut corners on sub-bases. For block paving driveways, we excavate to a depth of approximately 200mm–250mm to allow for a geotextile membrane, 150mm of compacted MOT Type 1 sub-base, sand laying course, and the 50mm/60mm block pavers. For porcelain patios, we excavate 150mm–200mm, applying a solid MOT base and a wet sand-and-cement mortar bed with priming slurry on the back of each tile.

Project duration varies based on scale. A standard 40-square-meter porcelain patio or driveway installation takes around 5 to 7 working days. Major foundations, structural concrete works, or site leveling projects range from 3 to 10 working days. We provide a projected schedule during our final proposal.

Absolutely. Asquith Groundworks carries full public liability insurance covering all excavations, heavy plant operations, foundations, drainage works, and paving. Our operators are fully licensed to ensure the highest safety standards on domestic and commercial sites.
