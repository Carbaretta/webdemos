What We Do

Our Services

A comprehensive range of gardening and outdoor services delivered to the highest standard across Peterborough.

Fencing

Supply and installation of all fence types — panel, post & rail, closeboard and more. Repairs and replacements also available.

Garden Waste Removal

Efficient collection and responsible disposal of all garden waste, including grass clippings, branches, soil and general green waste.

Garden Clearances

Full clearances of overgrown, neglected or end-of-tenancy gardens — stripped back, tidied and left ready for its next chapter.

De-Weeding

Thorough removal of weeds from borders, driveways, patios and lawns, keeping your garden clean, healthy and weed-free.

Turfing

Professional turf laying for a beautiful, even lawn — including ground preparation, levelling and premium-quality turf supply.

Decking

Design and construction of timber decking areas to create beautiful outdoor entertaining and relaxation spaces.

Timber Works

Bespoke timber structures and woodwork including pergolas, gates, garden furniture, log stores and more.

Raised Beds

Design and construction of timber raised beds for vegetables, herbs or flowers — perfect for both style and practicality.

Stump Removal

Complete stump grinding and removal, eliminating hazards and freeing up valuable garden space for planting or landscaping.

Tree Removal

Safe, professional removal of unwanted or dangerous trees of all sizes, with full clean-up and responsible timber disposal.
