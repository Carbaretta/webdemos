About DTS Joinery

Crafted with Passion,
Built to Last

DTS Joinery & Kitchen Fitting is a specialist carpentry and kitchen installation business based in Heckmondwike, West Yorkshire. We offer professional woodworking services to residential and commercial clients across Heckmondwike, Dewsbury, and the wider Kirklees area.

Our focus is simple: combining premium materials with precision craftsmanship to deliver finishes that exceed expectations. Whether it's a bespoke fitted wardrobe, internal doors, or a complete high-spec kitchen fitting, we handle every detail with care and attention.

We are fully insured, reliable, and work closely with our clients from initial concept to the final clean-up. We provide free, honest quotes and transparent timelines on all projects.
